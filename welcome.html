Mantra

Atha Yogānuśāsanam

ah-tah yo-gah-noo-shah-sah-nahm

"Now, the teaching of yoga begins."

The opening line of Patanjali's Yoga Sutras. One word — atha — means "now." Not when you're ready. Not when you've read enough. Now.

Neil Peacock

Yoga teacher, runner, surfer, from the land. Not the oracle.
